Bridge: Convert your mined DIL or DilV to wDIL or wDILV on Base

If you mine DIL or DilV and want to convert them to tradeable tokens on Base (Ethereum L2), use the form below. Your Dilithion node must be running on this computer.

DIL to wDIL
DilV to wDILV
What you need:
1. Your Dilithion (or DilV) node running and fully synced on this computer
2. Coins in your node wallet
3. A MetaMask wallet address — this is where your wrapped tokens will arrive on Base

Open MetaMask, make sure you're on Base network, and copy your address. Or click "Connect MetaMask" above.

Max per deposit: 500 DIL (daily limit: 1,000 DIL). Split larger amounts into multiple transactions.

After you click "Bridge Now", this page will connect to your node, select coins from your wallet, build the bridge transaction, sign it, and broadcast it. After 6 confirmations (~24 minutes for DIL), wDIL tokens will be minted to your MetaMask wallet automatically.

Important: Your node must be running with RPC enabled (the default). You need v3.6.9 or later for browser bridge support. Use the "Test Connection" button above to verify. If you have issues, ask in our Telegram or Discord.
